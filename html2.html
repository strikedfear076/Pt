Hope is a capacity to have an optimistic point of view on any circumstance.
For instance, given that we are in the midst of pandemic, one is hopeful if he/she believes that humanity can get through this heavy situation.
My drawing portrays the hand reaching out for hope that is the dove.
The hand is the symbol for humanity trying to grasp on the silver lining.
Because of all of the challenges to day, people are just try to grab on what ever they can just to have hope.
For me, this is my understanding about the word hope.
